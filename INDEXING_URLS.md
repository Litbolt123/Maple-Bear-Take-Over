2. **Paste into your indexing settings** - Add them one section at a time
3. **Test indexing** - Verify pages are actually indexed

## üìù Notes

- **URL encoding**: Some URLs have `%20` for spaces (like `Client%20Biomes`) - keep these as-is
- **Wildcards**: Some indexing systems support wildcards like `https://bedrock.dev/docs/stable/Entities/*` - check if yours does
- **Rate limiting**: Don't index too many pages at once - do it in batches
- **Version numbers**: Update version numbers in URLs when new versions are released
